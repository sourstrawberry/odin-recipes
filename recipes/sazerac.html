sazerac

a timeless nola classic

ingredients

  • one teaspoon sugar
  • three or four dashes peychaud's
  • a few drops water
  • two ounces rye whiskey
  • one teaspoon absinthe or pernod
  • lemon peel

steps

  1. chill and old fashioned glass in your freezer
  2. in a mixing glass, combine sugar, peychaud's, and few drops water. mix until sugar is dissolved, and add rye. add plenty of ice, and stir for thirty seconds.
  3. pour absinthe into your chilled glass, rotate glass until inside is well coated; discard the excess. strain the liquid from your mixing glass into serving glass. twist a piece of lemon peel over the drink.
